## Folder Governance
- **Purpose:** preserve historical evidence and superseded files.
- **What belongs here:** superseded drafts, superseded control files, retired prompt/blueprint artifacts.
- **What must not belong here:** active mandatory-read control law or implementation authority.
- **Authority boundary:** archive content is historical only and never outranks active control/source truth.
- **Update rules:** update this index when new historical sub-indexes are added.
- **No-go rules:** do not reactivate archived files as active authority without explicit migration.
- **Relationship to master index:** archive is mapped as historical-only in `control/02_MASTER_REPO_FILE_INDEX.md`.
