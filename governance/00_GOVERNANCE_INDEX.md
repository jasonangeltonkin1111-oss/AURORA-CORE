Governance index for schema contracts, registries, and examples.

## Folder Governance
- **Purpose:** define contract artifacts used by the system governance layer.
- **What belongs here:** schemas, registries, examples, proof-row contract artifacts.
- **What must not belong here:** repo run-discipline law, control-router duplication, implementation/runtime claims.
- **Authority boundary:** control/ owns process governance; this folder owns data/contract governance artifacts.
- **Update rules:** add/update indexes when schema/registry/example artifacts are added or retired.
- **No-go rules:** do not duplicate full docs doctrine here.
- **Relationship to master index:** folder authority and placement are tracked in `control/02_MASTER_REPO_FILE_INDEX.md`.
