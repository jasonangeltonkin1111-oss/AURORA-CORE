## Folder Governance
- **Purpose:** maintain prompt templates for workflow support.
- **What belongs here:** prompt templates and prompt indexes.
- **What must not belong here:** implementation/source-truth claims or duplicate control law.
- **Authority boundary:** prompts guide workflow but do not become implementation authority.
- **Update rules:** update prompt indexes when prompts are added/retired.
- **No-go rules:** do not claim runtime/trading readiness from prompt text.
- **Relationship to master index:** this folder is mapped from `control/02_MASTER_REPO_FILE_INDEX.md`.
